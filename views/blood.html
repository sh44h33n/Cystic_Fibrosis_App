Cystic Fibrosis

+ - How to check your Blood Pressure

Rise your arm to heart level

Wrap the cuff around the arm, Make sure the cuff is snug, not too tight

Place the wide head of the stethoscope on your arm

Tighten the valve and Inflate the cuff until the needle on the gauge reaches 180mmHg and then release the valve

Liaten to the stethoscope for a thumping sound. When you hear the first thump, note systolic blood pressure.

Keep watching the gauge and listen stethoscope. When hear only silence, note diastolic blood pressure.
